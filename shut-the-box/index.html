How to Play

Objective

Roll dice and flip down numbered tiles (1-9) that sum to your roll. Shut all 9 tiles for a perfect score. Lowest score wins.

Rules

  • On your turn, roll two dice. Flip any open tiles that add up to the total.
  • Once tiles 7, 8, and 9 are all shut, you may roll one die instead of two.
  • If no open tiles can match your roll total, your game ends. Your score = sum of remaining open tiles.
  • Score 0 = "Shut the Box."

Strategy

  • Target 7, 8, and 9 early to unlock single-die rolling.
  • When multiple combinations match a roll, prefer the one that shuts higher tiles.
  • Single-die rolling gives totals 1-6, which are easier to match late game.
  • Keep tile 1 open as long as possible for flexibility on odd-total rolls.

Common Mistakes

  • Always flipping just one matching tile when a higher-value combination is available.
  • Ignoring the one-die option once 7, 8, and 9 are shut.
  • Shutting tile 1 early when it could help later rolls.
