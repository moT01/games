How to Play

Objective

Collect more than 49 seeds in your store (the large pit on your right) before all houses are empty.

Taking a Turn

  • Pick up all seeds from one of your 7 houses
  • Drop one seed into each pit going counter-clockwise
  • Skip your opponent's store when passing it

Special Rules

  • Land in your store: take another turn
  • Land in your own empty house and the opposite house has seeds: capture both into your store
  • First move: both players choose simultaneously

Key Strategy

  • Houses close to your store give extra turns more easily
  • Set up captures by emptying a house, then sowing into it next turn
  • Count seeds before sowing to find extra-turn moves
